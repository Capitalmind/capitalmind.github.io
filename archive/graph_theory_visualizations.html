üîç BFS vs DFS Traversal

Traversal Order: Click a button to see traversal
Algorithm Comparison:
BFS: Explores level by level (breadth-first). Queue-based. Finds shortest path in unweighted graphs.
DFS: Explores as deep as possible first. Stack-based. Good for topological sorting and cycle detection.
